Recipe

Orzo Spinach Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small white onion, peeled and minced
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 cup diced celery
  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
  • 6 cups vegetable stock
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 cups whole wheat pasta
  • 4 cups spinach

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large stockpot ove medium-high heat.
  2. Add onion and saute for 4 minutes, until soft.
  3. Add carrots, celery and garlic and saute for an additional 3 minutes.
  4. Add vegetable stock, tomatoes, pasta, spices and stir to combine. Bring soup to a simmer, stirring occasionally.
  5. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until pasta is al dente.
  6. Stir in spinach and cook for 1-2 minutes until it is bright green and wilted. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
